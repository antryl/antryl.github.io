FAQ

Common questions

What founders and ops teams typically ask before we begin.

We already have a BI tool — do we still need you?
Tools don't design themselves. Most teams have Looker or Power BI but haven't defined what to measure or how to structure data correctly. We bring the strategy layer that makes the tools actually useful.
How do you handle sensitive business data?
We operate under NDA by default and work within your existing data access controls. We don't store your data externally — everything stays in your environment.
Can you work with non-technical teams?
Yes — this is where we excel. We translate complex analytics into plain business language and train your team on the outputs so they become self-sufficient.
Do you help with one-time projects or ongoing analysis?
Both. We do fixed-scope projects (e.g., "build us a growth dashboard") and ongoing retainers for teams who want a part-time analytics partner.
