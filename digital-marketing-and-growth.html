FAQ

Common questions

Things growth teams typically ask us before starting.

We already have a marketing agency. How do you fit in?
We complement agencies — they handle execution, we handle measurement and strategy. Many clients use us to hold their agency accountable with objective performance data.
Can you help if our tracking is a mess?
This is one of the most common things we fix. We audit your GA4, GTM, UTMs, and pixels, then clean up and standardise everything before building any analysis on top.
Do you run ads or just analyse them?
We focus on analytics and strategy, not execution. We tell you what to do and why — your team or agency handles the actual campaign management.
How quickly can we see results?
Most clients see actionable insights within 2 weeks and measurable improvement in CAC or ROAS within 60–90 days of implementing recommendations.
