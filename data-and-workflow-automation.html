FAQ

Common questions

Things clients usually ask before we get started.

How long does a typical project take?
Most automation and pipeline projects take 2–6 weeks depending on complexity. We'll scope this clearly in the discovery audit before any commitment.
Do I need a dedicated data team to work with you?
No. We work with founders, ops teams, and non-technical stakeholders. We handle the technical side and keep you updated in plain language throughout.
What if my data is messy or unstructured?
That's exactly where we start. Data cleanup, normalization, and quality assessment are built into our process — not billed separately.
Will I be locked into your infrastructure choices?
Never. We build with open tools and document everything so your internal team (or any future partner) can take over without dependency on us.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance?
Yes — we offer optional monthly retainers for monitoring, pipeline updates, and new data source integrations as your business grows.
