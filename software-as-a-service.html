FAQ

Common questions

Things founders and ops leads ask before getting started.

Do I need a technical co-founder or CTO to work with you?
No. We work directly with business owners and operators. We handle all technical decisions and explain tradeoffs in plain language so you stay in control without needing a technical background.
How long does it take to build an MVP?
Most MVPs take 4–10 weeks depending on scope. We define this clearly in the scoping session so there are no surprises — and we'll tell you if we think something is out of MVP scope.
What happens after launch — can we maintain it ourselves?
Yes. We document everything thoroughly and build with standard technologies specifically so your team (or any future developer) can take over. We also offer optional ongoing maintenance.
Can you work with our existing codebase?
Absolutely. We regularly extend, refactor, or build on top of existing systems. We do a code review at the start to understand what we're working with before scoping.
